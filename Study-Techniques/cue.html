Cue Cards 📝

Cue cards are a good way to test and improve memory by practicing information retreval. Cue cards are two sided with a question on one side, and an answer on the other.


  1. Write down the name of the concept or term on the front of the card, and the answer or definition on the back.
  2. Shuffle the deck, and try to answer what the front of the card is asking.
  3. After you attempted the question, turn over the card and see what you missed.
